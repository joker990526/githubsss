About Company

It is a young, successful and dynamically developing company that delivers a wide range of services in industrial and energetic construction. The Company is ready to fulfill the ambitious projects from the engineering to the construction of the facilities of atomic energy and oil & gas sector. Availability of highly-qualified engineering personnel, facilities & equipment is a main advantage of the Company on the construction market.

The basic priorities of the Company are three factors. They are as follows:

  • Health and Safety Management.
  • Quality Management.
  • Project Management.

Project Management

To ensure the activities are successfully fulfilled the Company has implemented a widely – recognized project management system (Project Management PM).

— Project Management process includes the planning and the tracking of all the work cycle from initial engineering stage to facility handover to Client. This process includes all the expenditure from financial expenses to procurement costs using risk factors and contingencies.

— In 2008 the Company’s employees took the training dedicated to the implementation of PM using the Microsoft Project Professional program product that was conducted by the regional Microsoft distributor.

— The results of PM implementation have shown that the use of this process enhances the work efficiency and simplifies communications between Client and Contractor.

— The carried out analyses of the PM application facilitate the use of the received information for potential projects to assess risks, contingencies and delays in material supply and work performance.
